Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The short version

learnjq.dev does not track you. No analytics, no cookies, no ads, no user accounts. We store as little data as technically possible.

Hosting

This site is hosted on infrastructure in Germany. When you visit, the server temporarily processes your IP address to deliver the page. Standard server logs (IP, timestamp, requested URL, HTTP status) may be retained for up to 7 days for security and debugging purposes. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure operation).

Local storage

learnjq uses your browser's localStorage to save your lesson progress and theme preference. This data never leaves your device — it is not sent to our server. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings or the "Reset" button on the site.

Cookies

learnjq does not set any cookies.

jq execution

When you run a jq filter, the JSON input and filter you typed are sent to our server for execution. This data is processed in memory only and is not stored or logged.

Third-party services

learnjq does not load any third-party resources — no external fonts, scripts, analytics, or tracking pixels.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. Since we don't collect personal data beyond temporary server logs, there is typically nothing to request. If you have questions, contact us at legal@mickhat.xyz.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. No email notifications since we don't have your email.
